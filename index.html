Frequently Asked Questions

You may fly any aircraft that Alaska Airlines operates.

X-Plane, P3D, FSX-FS20

You may transfer up to 125 hours with proof of hours included in your registration.

There is a federal law that prohibits us from collecting any personal information from anyone under the age of 13, including names.

No, we are not in any way affiliated with the real-world Alaska Airlines.

Since our VA operates under the VAMSYS system, we must abide and inherit all policies addressed in the VAMSYS Privacy Policy published at https://vamsys.io/legal/privacy. Therefore, we collect an exceedingly small amount of personal information on behalf of VAMSYS to conduct day-to-day operations at AlaskaVA. Information stored includes your real name and personal email address that can only be accessed by our executive staff. We will NOT share personal information outside of the AlaskaVA executives or VAMSYS unless given abundant permission by the given user.
